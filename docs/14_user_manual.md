# User Manual

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open the Kapok app
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your name, email, and password
4. Select your role:
   - **Team Member**: Join existing teams and complete tasks
   - **Team Leader**: Create and manage teams
5. Select your specialty (Medical, Engineering, etc.)
6. Tap **Create Account**

### Signing In

1. Open the app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Tap **Sign In**

### Forgot Password

1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link

---

## Teams

### Joining a Team (Team Members)

1. Go to the **Teams** tab
2. Tap **Join Team**
3. Enter the 6-character team code from your team leader
4. Tap **Join**

### Creating a Team (Team Leaders)

1. Go to the **Teams** tab
2. Tap **Create Team**
3. Enter a team name and optional description
4. Tap **Create**
5. Share the generated team code with your members

### Viewing Your Team

1. Go to the **Teams** tab
2. Tap on a team to see:
   - Team members
   - Team tasks
   - Team code (for leaders)

### Leaving a Team

1. Open the team detail page
2. Tap the menu icon (three dots)
3. Select **Leave Team**
4. Confirm your choice

---

## Tasks

### Viewing Tasks

1. Go to the **Tasks** tab
2. See all tasks for your teams
3. Use filters to find specific tasks:
   - Search by name
   - Filter by status (Pending, In Progress, Completed)
   - Filter by priority (Low, Medium, High)

### Creating a Task

1. Go to the **Tasks** tab
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Fill in:
   - Task title (required)
   - Description
   - Priority level
   - Location
   - Assignee (optional)
4. Tap **Save**

### Updating a Task

1. Tap on any task to open it
2. View task details including:
   - Description
   - Location on map
   - Assigned person
   - Status
3. Update status or details as needed

### Task Priorities

| Priority | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **High** | Urgent, address immediately |
| **Medium** | Important, address soon |
| **Low** | Can wait, address when possible |

### Task Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Not started |
| **In Progress** | Currently being worked on |
| **Completed** | Finished |

## Map

### Viewing the Map

1. Go to the **Map** tab
2. See tasks displayed as colored pins:
   - **Red**: High priority
   - **Orange**: Medium priority
   - **Green**: Low priority
   - **Gray**: Completed

### Using the Map Offline

The app automatically saves map tiles for your area. When you don't have internet:
- The map still works within ~3 miles of your last location
- Tasks you've already loaded are still visible
- New changes sync when you're back online

### Tapping a Task Pin

Tap any pin on the map to see task details and navigate to the full task view.

## Profile & Settings

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Go to the **Profile** tab
2. See your name, role, and account information

### Editing Your Profile

1. Go to **Profile** → **Edit Profile**
2. Update your name or specialty
3. Tap **Save Changes**

### Changing Settings

Go to **Profile** → **Settings** to:

- **Language**: Switch between English and Spanish
- **Theme**: Choose Light, Dark, or System
- **Location Services**: Enable/disable location access
- **Privacy**: Control analytics and crash reporting
- **Export Data**: Download your tasks and teams as a file

### Signing Out

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Sign Out**
4. Confirm your choice

---

## Working Offline

Kapok is designed to work without internet:

- **Tasks and teams** are saved on your device
- **Changes you make** are saved locally
- **When you reconnect**, everything syncs automatically

**Note**: Some actions require internet:
- Joining a new team
- Creating an account
- Password reset
