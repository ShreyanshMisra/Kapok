# Privacy Policy for Kapok

**Effective Date:** December 14, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 14, 2025

## Introduction

Kapok ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). The App is designed for disaster relief coordination and operates with an offline-first architecture.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you use Kapok, we collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide:

- **Account Information:** Email address, full name, and password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information:** Your selected role (e.g., Medical, Engineering, Construction, etc.)
- **Team Information:** Team names, team codes (for joining teams)
- **Task Information:** Tasks you create, including descriptions, locations, priorities, and assignments
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates when you create location-based tasks or use map features

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, actions performed, timestamps
- **Error Logs:** Crash reports and error messages to help us improve the App
- **Network Information:** Connection status (online/offline) for sync functionality

### Information Not Collected

We do NOT collect:
- Photos or media files (profile pictures use name initials only)
- Contacts from your device
- Call logs or SMS messages
- Microphone or camera data
- Background location data (location is only accessed when you actively use location features)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Core Functionality
- **User Authentication:** To create and manage your account
- **Team Coordination:** To enable team creation, joining, and management
- **Task Management:** To create, assign, track, and sync disaster relief tasks
- **Offline Synchronization:** To queue and sync data when you're offline and reconnect
- **Location Services:** To display tasks on maps and enable location-based coordination
- **Data Export:** To allow you to export your data in JSON format

### Service Improvement
- **Error Tracking:** To identify and fix bugs and crashes
- **Performance Monitoring:** To optimize app performance and reliability
- **Feature Development:** To understand which features are most useful

### Legal Compliance
- **Legal Obligations:** To comply with applicable laws and regulations
- **Safety:** To protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and others

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your data is stored locally on your device using Hive, a secure NoSQL database
- Data remains accessible even when offline
- Local data is encrypted at rest using standard device encryption

### Cloud Storage
- When online, data is synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Firebase servers are located in the United States
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- All network communications use TLS/SSL encryption
- Access to cloud data is restricted by authentication
- Regular security updates and patches

**However,** no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

- **Active Accounts:** As long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** 30 days after account deletion (for recovery purposes)
- **Offline Data:** Remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
- **Backup Data:** Up to 90 days in backup systems
- **Legal Holds:** Longer if required by law or legal process
